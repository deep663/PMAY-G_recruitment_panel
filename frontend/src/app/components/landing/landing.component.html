PMAY-G Recruitment 2022

Note

The candidate should be fluent in English and Hindi, apart from Assamese, as they will need to communicate with the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India.

State Program Manager

  • Minimum Qualification: B.Tech (Computer Science/IT/Civil/Electronics and Communication) or BE (Computer Science/IT/Civil/Electronics and Communication) or MBA (HR/Finance)
  • Experience: Minimum 7 years experience in Managerial Cadre in any public sector/reputed private sector organization, with IT knowledge.
  • Remuneration: Rs 75,000/- per month + Rs 500/- Travel Allowance + Rs 250/- for Telephone and Internet Charges
  • Maximum Age (as of 01.01.2022): 40 Years

State Program (Data Analysis and Executive Management)

  • Minimum Qualification: B.Tech (Computer Science/IT) or BE (Computer Science/IT) or MCA
  • Experience: Minimum 5 years experience in public sector/reputed private sector organization with IT knowledge.
  • Remuneration: Rs 50,000/- per month + Rs 500/- Travel Allowance + Rs 250/- for Telephone and Internet Charges
  • Maximum Age (as of 01.01.2022): 40 Years

IT Assistant

  • Minimum Qualification: BCA or BSc (IT)
  • Experience: Minimum 2 years experience in any public sector/private sector organization.
  • Remuneration: Rs 30,000/- per month + Rs 500/- Travel Allowance + Rs 250/- for Telephone and Internet Charges
  • Maximum Age (as of 01.01.2022): 30 Years
